About the Author

Leonard Diamond, M.D. wrote his first musical in college and continued writing through medical school, psychiatric residency and private practice, right up to today! Purim The Musical is one of his favorite works, as it connects his passion for playwrighting with his Jewish heritage. This piece is truly a breath of fresh air in a time where many Jews have lost their connection to their people's history. Purim The Musical offers an accessible and entertaining way to learn the Purim story for all ages and all people!

None of Dr. Leonard Diamond's professional nor musical feats would have been possible without the support of his wife Ruth of blessed memory. She was both the dream nurturer and realist who made the fulfillment of plays like Purim The Musical possible. Together, they have four children, eleven grandchildren, and ten great grandchildren!

Purim The Musical

Purim The Musical can be done either as a fully staged production or a radio play in front of a live studio audience. Please apply for the rights to perform if you are interested!
